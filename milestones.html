Project Proposal

Project proposal contains the research question that is proposed to address and what are the objectives need to be fulfil during the study.

    • Research Question - What is the problem we are willing address through this study
    • Background study – What are the related words and what the linkage to the proposed system is.
    • Research Gap – Identifying the gap we are willing to fill through the study, When compared to existing products and solutions
    • Solution – Proposed solution willing to provide through studying the research problem
    • Technologies – What are technologies willing to apply in the implementation of the product, and what is the capability of those technologies to provide the best solution.
    • Objectives of the Study – What are major and sub objectives willing to fulfil through the study
    • Business Value – Business aspect of the solution and what are the paths that this solution willing to bring revenue.

Deadline – 24th of March 2021

Allocated Marks – 15%
